    Painting Baxter

    About

    Autonomous robot that can detect surfaces to paint (i.e. walls) and have the ability cover all designated areas with paint using different brush sizes.

    Shopping List

    • 12 EXPO markers ($7.25) - Amazon
    • 17 x 23 inch Whiteboard ($12.39) - Amazon
    • 3 EXPO erasers ($5.99) - Amazon
